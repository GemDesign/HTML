Give her a call if you wish to talk about a new project that you would like to take to the next level in its Design or Development."
Gillian Powell Graphic Designer "Gillian graduated in 2009 with a BA in Multimedia from Cork Institute of Technology, and has a FETAC Level 5 in Graphic Design from Colaiste Stiofain Naofa. She has worked freelance for the last 4 years as a graphic and web designer and is proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Flash Actionscript, Final Cut Pro, and the Adobe Creative Suite."
Geraldine Twomey Photographer "I am very passionate about all aspects of Design. I have a background in Fine Art and specialised in Photography and image processing. I would describe myself now as a digital artist/photographer. My favourite programs to work with are Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign which collectively help me to bring the client’s vision to life and I can’t wait to start work with you."
Barry Delaney Web Developer "I am currently studying Business Information Systems at UCC and I will be in the third year of the course this year. Robin Screech Web Developer "My name is Robin and I am a graduate with a BA in Multimedia from Cork Institute of Technology with joint-accreditation from Hochschule Darmstadt in Germany.
